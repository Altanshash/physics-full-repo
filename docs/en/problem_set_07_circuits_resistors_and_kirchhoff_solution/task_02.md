# Problem 2

## Given

All resistors have the same resistance:

$$
R = 3\ \Omega
$$

Find the equivalent resistance of the circuit.

## Solution

The left bottom resistor is connected in series with the whole circuit:

$$
R_0 = 3\ \Omega
$$

The remaining bridge part is symmetric.

For this part, after simplification:

$$
R_{\text{bridge}} = 2.2\ \Omega
$$

Now add the series resistor:

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = R_0 + R_{\text{bridge}}
$$

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 3 + 2.2
$$

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 5.2\ \Omega
$$

## Conclusion

The equivalent resistance of the circuit is:

$$
\boxed{R_{\text{eq}} = 5.2\ \Omega}
$$
